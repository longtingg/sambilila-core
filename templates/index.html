Unified Architecture For
Zambian Academic Tenancies

Sambilila Core functions as a sovereign, multi-tenant learning management framework architected to serve consolidated environments across secondary and tertiary frameworks. Engineered explicitly to align with the Examination Council of Zambia (ECZ) and Ministry of Education benchmarks, our infrastructure isolates student registries, centralizes continuous assessments, and supplies interactive syllabus-indexed digital resources through structural, secure tokened access portals.
